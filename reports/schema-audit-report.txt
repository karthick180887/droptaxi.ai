--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Pages audited:       5 (Homepage, Route, City, About, Contact)
Overall grade:       C-
Critical errors:     8
Warnings:            12
Missing opportunities: 9

The site has schema markup on every page, which is a good starting point.
However, the implementation suffers from three systemic problems:

  1. WRONG DOMAIN: Every single URL in schema uses "seorankpro.in" instead of
     "droptaxi.ai". This is the single most damaging issue. Google will see a
     complete mismatch between the schema URLs and the actual page URLs.

  2. AUTO-GENERATED NONSENSE: The Phase2 schema blocks and several page schemas
     use auto-generated names like "About Us Taxi Service", "Reviews Taxi
     Service", and FAQ questions about "Book Your One Way Taxi". These look
     spammy to Google and provide zero value.

  3. INVALID JSON SYNTAX: Two Phase2 FAQPage blocks have broken JSON (missing
     opening quotes on "acceptedAnswer" keys). These will fail parsing entirely.

SCHEMA TYPES YOU ASKED ABOUT -- RECOMMENDATIONS
================================================================================

  LocalBusiness / TaxiService:
    VERDICT: Already present on homepage, but needs domain fix, @id, address,
    geo, and aggregateRating. TaxiService extends LocalBusiness, so using
    TaxiService alone is correct.

  FAQPage:
    VERDICT: DO NOT rely on this for rich results. FAQPage has been restricted
    to government and healthcare authority sites since August 2023. The existing
    FAQPage blocks will NOT generate SERP features for droptaxi.ai. Keeping
    well-written ones is harmless, but remove the auto-generated nonsense ones.

  BreadcrumbList:
    VERDICT: NEEDED on all inner pages. Currently only present (and broken) on
    homepage and contact page via Phase2 blocks. Route pages and city pages
    have HTML breadcrumbs but zero JSON-LD.

  WebSite with SearchAction:
    VERDICT: Already present on homepage. Just fix the domain URLs.

  Service for routes:
    VERDICT: Already present on route and city pages. Needs enrichment with
    description, better Offer details, and provider @id linking.

  Review / AggregateRating:
    VERDICT: MISSING and should be added. The site mentions "4.8/5 Average
    Service Rating" and has a dedicated reviews page. Add AggregateRating to
    the TaxiService entity.

  HowTo for booking process:
    VERDICT: DO NOT ADD. Google removed HowTo rich results in September 2023.
    HowTo schema will produce NO SERP features. The "How Booking Works" section
    on the homepage does not need schema markup.

  Product for car types:
    VERDICT: NOT RECOMMENDED. Car types (Sedan, SUV, Innova) are not products
    being sold -- they are vehicle options within a service. The existing
    Service + Offer pattern is the correct approach. If you want to differentiate
    pricing by car type, use multiple Offer entries within the Service.

DEPRECATED SCHEMA WARNING -- DO NOT ADD
================================================================================

You specifically asked about HowTo and Product schema. Here is why neither
should be used:

  HowTo:  Google removed HowTo rich results in September 2023. Adding HowTo
           schema to the "How Booking Works" section will produce ZERO rich
           result features. Do not add it.

  Product: Car types (Sedan, SUV, Innova Crysta) are not standalone products.
           They are vehicle options within a transportation service. Using
           Product schema here would be semantically incorrect and could
           trigger a manual action. Use multiple Offer entries within the
           Service schema instead (see Block 6 above).

  FAQPage: Already on several pages but will NOT generate rich results for
           this site. FAQPage has been restricted to government/healthcare
           authority sites since August 2023. Keep well-written ones if desired,
           but remove the auto-generated boilerplate ones.
